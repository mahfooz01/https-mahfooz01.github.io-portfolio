    How I work a ticket

    1. 01

      Diagnose

      Reproduce the issue, check logs, isolate whether it's hardware, software, or user error before touching anything.

    2. 02

      Resolve

      Apply the smallest fix that actually solves it — not the fastest one that just hides the symptom.

    3. 03

      Verify

      Confirm the fix holds under normal use, not just right after the reboot.

    4. 04

      Document

      Write it up so the next person — often future me — doesn't have to start from zero.
